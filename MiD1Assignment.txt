UNIT – 1
CLOUD PRINCIPLES & WELL-ARCHITECTED FRAMEWORK
================================================================================
[Fundamental Principles of Cloud Computing | Shared Responsibility Model |
 Cloud-Native Architecture | Well-Architected Framework | Best Practices for
 Designing Scalable Cloud Applications]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.1                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Explain the fundamental principles of cloud computing:
    On-demand Self-service, Broad Network Access, Resource Pooling,
    Rapid Elasticity and Measured Service.                               [4]
(b) Discuss how Rapid Elasticity and Measured Service help organizations
    improve performance and control cost compared to traditional
    on-premises computing.                                               [3]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.2                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) What is the Shared Responsibility Model in cloud computing?
    Differentiate the responsibilities of the cloud service provider and
    the customer.                                                        [3]

    suitable examples. Explain one security risk that arises if this
    model is misunderstood.                                              [4]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.3                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    characteristics (for example: microservices, containers, managed
    services, API-driven design, externalized state, automation).        [4]

    cloud-native application with respect to scalability and failure
    handling.                                                            [3]

Q.4                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Reliability, Performance, Cost and Sustainability. Explain any three

(b) Write any three best practices for designing scalable cloud
    applications.                                                        [3]

UNIT – 2
DISASTER RECOVERY IN CLOUD
================================================================================
[Concepts of Backup & Disaster Recovery | High Availability & Fault
 Tolerance | Disaster Recovery Strategies (RPO & RTO) | Cloud-Based Backup
 Solutions & Snapshots | Case Studies on Disaster Recovery Implementation]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.5                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Define the following terms:
    (i) Backup  (ii) Disaster  (iii) Disaster Recovery
    (iv) Business Continuity                                             [3]
(b) Justify the statement: “Backup alone is not Disaster Recovery.”
    Support your answer with the need for runbooks, failover /
    rebuild procedures and restore testing.                              [4]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.6                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Differentiate between High Availability and Fault Tolerance with
    suitable cloud examples.                                             [3]
(b) Explain Single Point of Failure (SPOF). How do redundancy, load
    balancing, health checks and multi-zone deployment help in reducing
    SPOF in a cloud application?                                         [4]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.7                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Define RTO (Recovery Time Objective) and RPO (Recovery Point
    Objective). Explain each with one real-life example.                 [3]
(b) Explain the following Disaster Recovery strategies:
    Backup & Restore, Pilot Light, Warm Standby, Hot Standby and
    Multi-site Active-Active.
    Compare any two strategies with respect to cost, RTO and RPO.        [4]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q.8                                                                      [7]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
(a) Describe cloud-based backup solutions and snapshots. Explain the
    use of disk/VM snapshots, object-storage versioning and managed
    database backups with point-in-time recovery. Why is restore
    testing important?                                                   [4]
(b) Discuss any one case study on Disaster Recovery implementation.
    Identify the problem, suggest a suitable DR strategy and justify
    it using RTO and RPO.                                                [3]
